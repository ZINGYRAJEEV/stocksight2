// Competitive Advantage
🛡️
Your Defensible Moat
🇮🇳
India-Specific Data Quirks
Circuit breakers, T+1 settlement, SEBI regulations, FII/DII flow data, Nifty-specific volatility patterns — invisible to Western tools.
📄
Broker Statement Parser
Auto-import from Zerodha, Groww, Upstox PDFs. A painful integration that creates high switching cost once users have their data in.
🧾
STCG/LTCG Tax Engine
Absolutely no free tool calculates Indian capital gains tax correctly. This single feature justifies ₹499/year on its own.
📈
Indian Stress Scenarios
Pre-built crash scenarios using actual NSE data: 2008 crisis, COVID March 2020, 2018 IL&FS, NBFC crisis — not US market events.
